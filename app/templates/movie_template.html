CAPTAIN AMERICA: THE WINTER SOLDIER







Movie Description



As Steve Rogers struggles to embrace his role in the modern world, he teams up with a fellow Avenger
and S.H.I.E.L.D agent, Black Widow, to battle a new threat from history: an assassin known as the Winter Soldier.

Director/s: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
Writer/s: Christopher Markus, Stephen McFeely
Actors: Chris Evans, Samuel L. Jackson, Scarlett Johansson, Robert Redford, Sebastian Stan, Anthony Mackie
Runtime: 136 min
Release date: 4 April 2014 (USA)
Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
Certificate: PG-13
